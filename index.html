eCW Fax Intake, Document Labeling & Routing Standards

Purpose:
Standardize how incoming faxes are handled, including when to route as a document, when to create a TE, who labels and closes documents, and what information must be captured before provider review.
Golden Rule:
Route fast. Do not interpret outside of your role. When a TE is required, include only the minimum reason and assign correctly.

Reviewed Team: Document Insight (Required Fields)

Reviewed Team Responsibility:
Relabel and close documents. When a TE exists, add Document Insight into the TE so the TE Team can route without rework.

Required Document Insight Block (Paste into TE)

DOCUMENT INSIGHT (Reviewed Team)
- Fax Type:
- Key Summary (1–2 lines):
- Action Needed: YES / NO
- Who Owns Next Step: Provider / Clinic Staff / Clinical Admin / OM / Referrals / Med PA / Imaging PA / DME
- Due Date / Timeframe (if stated):
- Urgency: Routine / Time-Sensitive / STAT
- Provider FYI Needed: YES / NO

Field Rules

  • Key Summary stays 1–2 lines. No copy/paste dumping.
  • Action Needed must be explicitly YES or NO.
  • Due Date / Timeframe is required if mentioned anywhere in the fax.
  • Urgency must be Time-Sensitive when deadlines, discharge follow-up, pre-op dates, or peer-to-peer windows exist.
  • Provider FYI Needed = YES only when provider review/decision is required.
Role Summary:
Delegators route. Reviewed Team labels and summarizes when needed. Clinical Admin executes and assigns.
